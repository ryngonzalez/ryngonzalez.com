**[0001 Kathryn Gonzalez](https://podcasters.spotify.com/pod/show/design-spotlight/episodes/0001-Kathryn-Gonzalez-equftf)**

Kathryn Gonzalez is Head of Design Infrastructure at DoorDash as well as an iOS/Web engineer and Design Systems Designer. In this Spotlight we talk about how a design infrastructure team gets built and the challenges with growing a team that can design infrastructure across many diverse platforms.

[Follow](https://podcasters.spotify.com/pod/show/design-spotlight/episodes/0001-Kathryn-Gonzalez-equftf) [Kathryn on Twitter](https://twitter.com/ryngonzalez) at @ryngonzalez or learn more about DoorDash design at [doordash.design](http://doordash.design) (they're hiring).
